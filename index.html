Who I Am

I am an experimental researcher working at the frontier of quantum photonics and 2D materials physics. Currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at CEMES & LPCNO (Toulouse), my work spans the full pipeline — from nanofabrication and heterostructure assembly to cryogenic optical spectroscopy and quantum light characterisation.

My doctoral research at CNRS-LNCMI and Paul Sabatier University focused on magneto-optical spectroscopy of TMDC heterostructures under extreme magnetic fields up to 70 T, revealing twist-angle-dependent exciton physics and Moiré effects.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between fundamental quantum optics and device-level photonic integration — building tomorrow's quantum technologies one photon at a time.

Key Research Projects

NV-Center Quantum Sensing & Dielectric Nanoantenna Integration
AFM Nanoxerography-based deterministic placement of nanodiamonds with NV centers into dielectric nanoantennas and metasurfaces. g²(τ) second-order autocorrelation measurements confirm single-photon purity. Fourier-plane imaging used for far-field emission pattern mapping.
NV Centersg²(τ) HBTAFM NanoxerographySingle-Photon EmissionMetasurfaces
k-Space Vector Filtering for Spin-Dark/Bright Exciton Separation in TMDCs
Fourier-plane (back-focal-plane) microscopy with engineered spatial frequency masks and central beam-block aperture stops to selectively transmit in-plane (σ±, TE, bright) or out-of-plane (z-polarised, TM-mode, spin-dark) dipole emission lobes. Exploiting K/K′ valley angular momentum selection rules in atomically thin MoS₂ to map emission anisotropy in reciprocal space, disentangle momentum-dark and spin-dark exciton contributions, and extract valley polarisation degree as a function of in-plane wavevector |k∥|. Room-temperature spin-dark excitonic emission enabled by high-NA objective (NA > 1.4) and sub-λ aperture engineering.

TMDC Heterostructure Fabrication & AFM Ironing
Novel fabrication route for MoSe₂/MoS₂ heterostructures with AFM ironing post-processing to flatten bubbles and enhance PL quantum yield. Revealed twist-angle-dependent dehybridization of momentum-indirect interlayer excitons.

Ultrafast Charge Transfer in Perovskite-TMDC Heterostructures
Polarised pump-probe and transient absorption spectroscopy on WSe₂-BAPI hybrid heterostructures. Sub-picosecond charge transfer via Dexter-like coupling and emergence of new excitonic species at the organic-2D interface.
